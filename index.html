The Most Influential Award Series

Oyo state most influential award series provides a unique opportunity to meet and connect with notable people in the State, from different categories.

The Most Influential Award Series

Categories

1

100 Most Influential Personalities & Brands Award

i. Most Influential Personalities, emanating under the following Nine (9)categories; (Politics & Governance, Innovators, Tech, Entrepreneurs, Journalists/Broadcasters, Entertainment, New Media, NGO/Orphanage Homes, and Sports category.

ii. Most Influential Brands, arising from the following categories; Education, Finance, Media, Fashion, Food & Restaurants, and Others.

Nominate

2

Oyo State Youth of the Year Award

This is a voting category that celebrate a winner after a voting process from a 10 nominees across all the sectors from Oyo State, who are versatile, energetic and productive youth below the age of 40 years.

Nominate

2

Oyo State Woman of the Year Award

This is another voting category that celebrate a female winner after a voting process from a 10 nominees, the nominees are drowned from different sector of the State and whose are successful business owner or politician. The age limit for this category is from 40 years and above, the nominees can be an origin/residents of Oyo State, and can even own businesses in the State.

Nominate

2

Oyo State Man of the Year Award

Another attention-grabbing voting category that celebrate a man winner after a transparent voting process, which includes 10 nominees, the nominees take account from part of the State and whose are successful business owner or politician. The age limit for this category is exactly same with the woman of the year category, age 40 and above, the nominees can also be an origin/residents of Oyo State, and can even be a business owner in the State.
